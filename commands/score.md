description: Get ENERGY STAR score and benchmarking metrics for a property
argument-hint: [property name or ID, or "list shared properties first"]

Retrieve ENERGY STAR benchmarking data for this property:
1. Call list_shared_properties to confirm the property is connected
2. Call get_metrics to retrieve: ENERGY STAR score, site EUI, source EUI, GHG emissions
3. Interpret: how does this score compare to the national median? Is it eligible for certification?
